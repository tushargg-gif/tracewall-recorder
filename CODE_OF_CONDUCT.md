# Code of Conduct

AgentProof Recorder should be a practical, respectful space for people working on agent reliability and developer tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Focus criticism on code, docs, tests, and product behavior.
- Assume good intent, but be specific when something is wrong.
- Keep discussions technical and evidence-based.
- Respect privacy. Do not post secrets, private code, or sensitive run evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Spam or repeated off-topic promotion
- Sharing credentials, customer data, or sensitive logs

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block accounts that repeatedly violate this code of conduct.

For sensitive issues, use the guidance in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
